REMOTE LOCK

The lock engages a bolt inside the door. This piece of metal extends from the door into the frame and holds it closed. There are two main styles of latch (or bolt)—a spring bolt and a deadbolt. Spring Bolt – A spring latch is a bolt held in place by a spring clip.
